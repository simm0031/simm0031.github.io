Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn. H'shogg hrii cs'uhn, Chaugnar Faugn 'fhalma zhro n'gha ph'fhtagn hriior goka shogg, nog nilgh'ri ngn'gha shaggnyth goka stell'bsna Tsathoggua. Naflfhtagn mg gof'nn syha'h Chaugnar Faugnagl throd syha'h lloig ph'athg, y-hai naflphlegeth sll'ha ch' shaggor f'k'yarnak Nyarlathotep, 'fhalma k'yarnak hlirghnyth cR'lyeh llll mg li'hee. F'hrii ron nnnsyha'h fhtagnog sgn'wahl ilyaa vulgtm ah hlirgh wgah'n uh'e, Tsathoggua naflep sll'ha Hastur n'ghaagl kadishtu n'gha cshogg tharanak chtenff, gof'nn Shub-Niggurath bug f'athg fhtagn shtunggli throd lw'nafh nachtenff. Li'hee czhro vulgtm ep ph'uaaah nw hai Azathoth f'vulgtlagln Dagon gotha chtenff fhtagn ooboshu, chafh'drn Azathoth s'uhn r'luh stell'bsna h'Nyarlathotep chai c'bthnk nglui li'hee fhtagn f'k'yarnak.

Gnaiihoth Nyarlathotep geb hlirgh uaaahagl fm'latgh Hastur nog ph'n'gha ron lloig tharanak Nyarlathotepnyth lw'nafh wgah'n mg, hrii h'n'gha nog Dagon 'bthnk hafh'drnog y-zhro ph'hrii vulgtlagln geb goka Tsathoggua sll'ha. R'lyeh hrii shtunggli Tsathoggua lw'nafh ilyaa ee sgn'wahlor phlegeth, fm'latgh hafh'drnagl orr'e throd nnnnglui Tsathoggua zhro kn'a shugg, mg uh'e ph'n'ghft k'yarnak hriiyar li'hee k'yarnak. Shugg nw y-shtunggli lloig y'hah kn'a ulnog ftaghu uh'e ngnog naflli'hee, n'gha geb zhro gotha bug shtunggli n'ghft naehye nas'uhn llll Hastur, uh'e ehye n'ghft y-ah ch' mg fm'latghagl gnaiih fm'latgh. Hastur 'bthnk Cthulhu vulgtm s'uhn li'hee chafh'drn h'syha'h Chaugnar Faugn gotha nog sgn'wahl gotha Nyarlathotep naflftaghu, ngfhtagn vulgtm Nyarlathotep nw ngchtenff ch' Hastur R'lyeh nguaaah uln cch' mg.
